MIS3690 (Web Tech) Project Proposal

Spring 2021

Abegail Santos

  1. What is your website about?

    My project will be about Algo Filipino's U.S.A. Chapter. Algo Filipino is a non-profit organization that I'm currently involved in. Its main mission is to increase the accessibility of technology education among the Filipino youth by promoting the native language as the primary mode of learning. The main chapter is based in the Philippines, but we are looking to expand to the U.S.A.

  2. What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

    The objective of my website is to inform people about Algo Filipino's mission and vision, current projects and programs, and how they can become involved in the organization. Currently, Algo Filipino only has Facebook, Instagram, and Linkedin pages as a way to promote the organization. Developing a website for the chapter will therefore be important in establishing our credibility and furthering our online presence.

  3. Who is your target audience set?

    The target audience for this website will be Filipino and Filipino-American High School and College students in the United States who are interested in entering the field of technology, learning about the native language, or becoming involved in a youth organization operating in the Philippines.

  4. What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

    1. Homepage: This page will include our mission and vision, an overview of what we do in the organization, and the story behind its establishment.

    2. Programs: This page will provide more details on each of our programs

    3. The state of technology in the Philippines: This page will provide a background of the state of technology in the Philippines, and explain why making technology education accessible is important now more than ever. This will educate Filipino and Filipino-Americans students in America who are not aware of the challenges in a third-world country but would like to contribute to our mission.

    4. Meet the team: This page will include the name and pictures of our team members.

    5. How to become a volunteer/core member: This page will provide the necessary steps that interested applicants should take if they want to become part of our team.

    6. Contact us

    7. Sitemap
